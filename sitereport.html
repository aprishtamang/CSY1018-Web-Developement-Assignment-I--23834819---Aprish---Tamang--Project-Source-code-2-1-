My Website Development Experiences

I am grateful to NAMI college and my module tutor,
Chirag Thapa, for providing me with the opportunity to enhance my web development skills.
I had my first experience with coding and web development in 2018 when I learned Graphics designing, HTML, CSS.
Initially, I faced some difficulties, but with time, I learned a lot and became more confident in coding.
In 2018, during my +2 studies, I had a lesson on web designing, and I created a website for my dad business.
Then in 2020, during my 12th grade, I learned C language, c++, which was new to me, and I found it more complicated than HTML and CSS,
but still, I quickly grasped it because of my coding knowledge in different p-languages. I even scored good marks during my examination.
However, due to the COVID 19 pandemic, I had to take a break. After the pandemic was controlled,I applied to USA for my further studies but the interviewer did not give me the visa. I was so confused during that time what to do and what not to,and one of my friend suggested me
to join NAMI college then, I joined NAMI college to pursue a bachelor's degree. The environment of the college is peaceful and fulfilling,
just as my friend mentioned. The Web Development module (CSY1018) taught me HTML, CSS, Git, and GitHub,
which were all new to me. Though Git and GitHub seemed daunting at first, they turned out to be easy and fun
to use after some time. I found that display: Grid was a new feature that I had never used before,
and it was more complex than float and flex, but it was more convenient and faster.
My favorite colors are Blue,white, yellow and green and I choose them for main work in my webpages. I often browsed Pinterest and Google for designing my webpage. I visited my sites through which
i can get few knowledge about designing and viewed various templates online.
